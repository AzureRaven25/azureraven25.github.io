CompE @ Georgia Tech

My journey into technology started with a simple curiosity about how things work under the hood, and that curiosity is what ultimately led me to study Computer Engineering at Georgia Tech. I have always believed that the best software is not just functional - it is resilient and thoughtfully built from the ground up.

Whether I am hardening a Linux system against vulnerabilities or architecting the backend logic for a full-stack web application, I am driven by the challenge of making complex systems feel seamless and secure for the person on the other side of the screen.

For me, engineering is about more than writing code. It is about the discipline of creating tools that people can actually trust. I grew up in Lilburn, GA, and that grounded, community-driven mindset carries into everything I build.
